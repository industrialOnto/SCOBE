Introduction back to ToC

The development of SCOBE considers two complementary meanings of the benchmarking concept. One of them is benchmarking as an improvement project based on the comparison of the performance of supply chain and its components with respect to other equivalent entities. This is referred as benchmarking of reference values. The other one corresponds to benchmarking based on the analysis and implementation of certain practices that are adopted to reach good performance according to certain criteria. This is referred a benchmarking of good practices. In both cases, reference points, called benchmarks, against which contrast/analysis is made are identified.
